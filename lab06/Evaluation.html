Evaluation of Risk of Rain Website

The Risk of Rain website has 3 Critical design issues.

  1. It doesn't meet the WCAG 2 AA minimum contrast ratio thresholds
  2. img elements don't have alternate text or a role of none or presentation
  3. Doesn't ensure links have discernible text

Who is the site's target audience?

Fans of the game Risk of rain and its sequal or those who might want to purchase the game

How is the site organized?

The site organization is linear based around the release date of content related to the game

Which CRAP Design Principle does the use?

The Risk of Rain website uses a CRAP Design Principle, which is the use of color constrast to make the clickable elements more visually distinct. It also follows the Repetition principle by having a consitient design throughout the site.
