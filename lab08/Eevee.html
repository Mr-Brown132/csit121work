Eevee Fun Facts

  • Eevee is the Evolution Pokemon, known for their ablity to evole into 8 differnt froms of Pokemon.
  • They are a well liked Pokemon that symbolizes endless change and possiblity.
  • In Eevee's latest pokedex entry, "Its ability to evolve into many forms allows it to adapt smoothly and perfectly to any environment.".
  • Eevee is a normal type pokemon. The 8 forms that They can Evolve into are: Vaporeon, Jolteon, Espeon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Glaceon, Flareon, and Sylveon.
  • Its evolutions are lovingly called eeveelutions by the pokemon community
  • Eevee evolves into Vaporeon when coming into contact with a water stone
  • Eevee evolves into Jolteon when coming into contact with a thunderstone
  • Eevee evolves into Flareon when coming into contact with a firestone
  • Eevee evolves into Umbreon when extremly happy during the nighttime
  • Eevee evolves into Leafeon when around a moss rock
  • Eevee evolves into Glaceon when around a ice rock
  • Eevee evolves into Espeon when extremly happy during the daytime
  • Eevee evolves into Sylveon when extremely happy while knowing a fairy type move
