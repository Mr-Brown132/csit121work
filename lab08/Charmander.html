Charmander Fun Facts

  • Charmander is the Lizard pokemon
  • It is one of the very first pokemon to be created
  • Charmander is a fire type pokemon
  • Its Evolution Charizard is one of the most well known pokemon most likely only behind Pikachu
  • It was in the very first pokemon games red and blue
  • The flame on Charmander's tail is a symbol of its life force
  • When the flame goes out, Charmander is no longer alive
  • Charmander is the first in the evolutionary line
  • Evoles first into Charmeleon
  • Charmeleon then Evolves into Charizard
  • Charmander is a combination of Char and salamander with Char being another word for burnt
  • Charmander's final evolution is Charizard isn't a dragon type even though it looks like one
